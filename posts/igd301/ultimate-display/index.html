Ultimate Display

February 3, 2022

This is how Sutherlands describes the “Ultimate Display”, in his paper from 1965 : “a room within which the computer can control the existence of matter”.

Often we think about Mixed Reality experiences by placing them on the Milgram continuum, going from reality to virtual reality.

But how would we place Sutherland’s Ultimate Display on this continuum ?

This idea actually goes out of the scope of the continuum. The computer is not adding virtual elements to the real world, but modifying reality itself. It is not stimulating human receptors to emulate sensations, but actually creating the sensations by creating real world elements.

Either this Ultimate Display is not even Mixed Reality anymore (it’s just pure reality : one cannot put a chair in a room and decide it is virtual) or it covers the whole continuum at once.
